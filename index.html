    Who we are


    “NeoTek is an applications and software development firm committed to the design and development of technology for personal and enterprise support through the delivery of value driven IT based products, services and tools. We were found in February 2017 majorly focus in the development of Telecoms & Digitally driven platforms for media houses, VAS Aggregators & Financial Institutions.”

    Our Mission



    “Drive growth by innovation – we exist for the sole purpose of harnessing the true potential of information systems as both a personalized mass engagement tool and also a support system for enterprise upward mobility.”


    Our Vision



    “Our vision is to be the front runner as regards the design and creation of new technology that supports and empowers people and their businesses in their daily lifestyles, towards attaining their fullest potential.”

    What we do

    We specialize in deployment of end-to-end solutions that sees us utilize our skills in research & design, Agile Project Management, Application Development, Payments (or Micropayment/VAS Billing), testing and provisioning in a live/production environment - We provide services that brings together actors in the value chain, content and the most suitable technology for the respective actors all of which facilitates an enhanced experience for our users and clients respectively. Also amongst our service offerings is mobile aggregation through the Nigerian mobile networks. We are uniquely positioned for aggregation service for 3rd party wireless application service providers (WASPs) and content providers (CP) to connect and deploy mobile services on the networks.



    Our Services



    IT Advisory & Business Research

    We help design tailored solution architectures for your Business support systems and also assist in drafting business case documentation or Business Justification for any of your new or existing products and help Identify strategies that fit with the product and its supporting offerings..





    Marketing & Branding

    We assist individuals, small businesses and enterprises in conducting marketing campaigns ranging from outdoor activations, outdoor advertising, Direct Marketing; Bulk SMS – which we also provide to enterprises for communication with their customers and delivery of notifications – known for the most cost effective means to disseminate information.





    Software & Applications Development (Web, Mobile & Desktop)

    We help design low cost and highly scalable software/applications for small scale enterprises and are also highly competent in delivering high-end and robust enterprise solutions ranging from websites, mobile apps (iOS, Android, Windows) all of which are also highly optimized for mobile browser usage.





    Digital & Social Media Management

    We assist corporations and enterprises in managing their digital presence for a reasonably low retainer fee that Is tailored to the scale of the client and the depth of their needs.





    SMS/VAS Services

    We help aggregate SMS voting, bulk SMS, and mobile services via 3rd party wireless application providers (WASPs) and content providers (CP), helping them deploy services on the networks.
